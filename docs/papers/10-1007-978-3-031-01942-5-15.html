Keywords: Social networks, Trace, Indicators, Metrics, Trace-based system

Facebook presents the largest social network on the Internet. Users’ interaction traces seem to provide a good basis for analyzing this environment. Several methods and approaches have proposed to treat different questions (categorization, social relations detection, behavior evaluation, etc.). However, the existing methods cannot satisfy users’ requirements. In this paper, we propose a new approach to detect the social pole in Facebook. Our system measures the effectiveness of a user in the social network using the technics of the Trace-Based System. It aims to collect and analyze user’ interaction traces over the Facebook platform then transform them into indicators to evaluate a list of categorization metrics. Finally, we evaluate our approach with a case study. We suggest a list of metrics and evaluate each metric by a list of indicators. Every indicator has calculated from a list of traces (observed elements).
