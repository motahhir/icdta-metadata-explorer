Keywords: Sustainable urban logistics, Literature review, Bibliographic study

Currently, we observe large numbers associated with internal urban migration exacerbating the environmental urban scenario, which is the outcome of various prior circumstances, and this is why urbanization is becoming more widespread. This is true of internet shopping, globalization, and damaging industrial and human resources, to name a few examples. The key solutions are primarily involved with urban mobility, the involvement of the relevant actors, trip optimization, the usage of lanes and roads, and vehicle selection. One of the most significant foundations of the notion of “urban logistics” is sustainability, which is one of the main parts of flow management. Indeed, the ultimate goal extends beyond the delivery of commodities, promoting the environment as a vital component of urban logistics. We will attempt to solve the following problem: Where are we in terms of urban logistics? To address this issue, we used the following strategy: Presentation of the fundamental principles, selection of articles to be used in the study, identification of the major axes, actors, and clarification of the most important axis.
