Keywords: Artificial intelligence, Education, Resource optimization, Data analytics, Organizational management

Artificial intelligence has had a profound effect how education and organizational systems operate. The present study is to explore the impact of AI on enterprise and educational processes, in the hope that it can contribute to more sustainable changes. Through the application of AI, resource management and decision-making at educational institutions or organizations may turn out be simple and easy the study appraises the data of a broad spectrum of professionals in various sectors, employing statistical tools such as Principal Component Analysis (PCA) and quantitative methods. The conclusion drawn is that AI can make individualized instruction a reality, has improved efficiency in organizations and provides managers of educational resources with an easier way of doing business. The consistency coefficients, such as Cronbach's alpha, also confirmed the model employed in this study. Nevertheless, it also points out some major problems facing educators and students as well as other members of society: technology gaps among different groups, shifting amounts of information which need to be managed on an ongoing basis in all educational organizations and resistance to changes in pedagogical forms adopted. Last but not least, future tasks include expanding the training of AI for teachers and improving the digital infrastructure so that these technologies can be applied equitably and successfully to education. In conclusion, while AI holds great promise for improvements in education systems as well as organizational processes, there continue several barriers which must be overcome if all of its benefits are to be realized.
