Technological and Entrepreneurial Factors as Drivers of Private Equity Funding in Morocco

Keywords: ARDL, Entrepreneurship, Private equity, Technology

This paper investigates technological and entrepreneurial factors as drivers of private equity fundraising in Morocco. Based on ARDL analysis, the results of this research indicate that, in the short run, only productivity has a significant and negative effect on fundraising. Then, patents have no significant impact in the long run, while the number of new businesses, productivity, research, and development has a considerable effect. Finally, these findings demonstrated that the government must enhance the entrepreneurial ecosystem to promote private equity investments.
