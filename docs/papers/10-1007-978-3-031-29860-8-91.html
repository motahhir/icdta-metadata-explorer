Prediction and Optimization of Uber Services: A Case Study of Morocco

Keywords: Uber, Optimization, Prediction, Machine learning, Smart mobility

Uber is an intelligent mobility tool for reserving vehicles and optimizing customer journeys according to their needs. In this article, our goal is to optimize the purchase of vehicles. The optimization solution adopted is based on the prediction of the number of customers and can therefore be easily adapted in the event of a change in demand. Our approach is being tested in Morocco, which is an emerging developing country that is increasingly adopting and encouraging smart mobility tools. The experimental results show the promise of the method, which can be generalized to other case studies.
