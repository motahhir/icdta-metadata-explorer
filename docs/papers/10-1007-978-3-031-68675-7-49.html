Keywords: Digital Healthcare Transformation, Artificial Intelligence, Slavoj Zizek, Authentic Act, Psychoanalytic Map

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is an emerging technology that transforms healthcare management. AI solutions reduce time and make patient-doctor interactions efficient. This paper explores a compelling intersection between digital transformation, Artificial Intelligence, and Zizek’s philosophical insights. Psychoanalytic mapping offers an innovative tool for decision-making in AI applications. This new tool emerges from an authentic act and develops more ethical ways to use AI.
