Keywords: Managerial and technological innovation, Hospital performance, Public hospital, Universal Health Coverage (UHC), Integrative performance model

At present, health care system in Morocco is undergoing an unprecedented reform where the Generalization of Medical Coverage (GCM) is the most structured. As part of this health system reform, the hospital is undergoing profound changes to innovate its system and to perform well because without an efficient hospital, universal health coverage, with a social objective, would be ineffective. Besides, health coverage wouldn’t succeed without an efficient hospital that follow such a change by its creativity and its organizational and technological innovation. It is, therefore, appropriate to measure the perception of hospital performance in the context of organizational change and to assess how this change may affect the dynamics of technological and managerial innovation.
