Keywords: DFIG, FOC, DPC, WECS

This study proposes two different controlling approaches of Wind Energy Conversion System (WECS). The first one deals with Field/Oriented/Control/ (FOC) and the second with Direct Power Control (DPC). Both approaches were applied on Doubly-Fed-Induction-Generator-(DFIG). DPC seems to give good tracking and better efficiency compare to FOC. In fact, by comparing Stator active and reactive powers, DPC gives much less oscillations. Regarding rotor and stator currents, DPC needs less time to achieve steady-state.
