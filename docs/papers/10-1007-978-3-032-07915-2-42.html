Keywords: Healthcare sector, Smart finance, Artificial intelligence, Market efficiency first section

The convergence of efficient financial administration of interconnected healthcare represents a revolutionary development in modern civilization, blending once separate domains into a symbiotic environment. The unity, which is aimed at enhancing human health via mobile devices and intelligent sensors, while streamlining financial procedures with advanced advances such as artificial intelligence (AI), has the potential to change healthcare views and decisions regarding finances. Nevertheless, when these realms mix, apprehensions over anonymity, ethics, and security arise, demanding the need for ethical use of technology. The extensive analysis, using the principal component approach the examining several interrelated variables, highlights the complex connections between innovation in technology, monetary regulation, and country financial performance. The findings, verified using Smart PLS, confirm the assumptions pertaining to scientific expenditure, client fulfillment, GDP expansion, and general prosperity. The research highlights the necessity of addressing economic disparities while building an economy ecology one is both efficient and morally upright.
