Optimization of a Grid-Connected PV System for a Moroccan Industrial Unit Using PVsyst

Keywords: grid-connected, PV system, PVsyst software, green industry unit

This paper investigates the feasibility of integrating a grid-connected PV system for an industrial factory to provide clean energy using PVsyst software. The PV system aims to reduce the use of the distribution grid when load demand reaches its peak value. The system’s sizing is done based on theoretical and simulation methods. Results show that the proposed system can cover 80% of the daily consumed energy. The performance ratio of the system is 0.84 while the energy injected into the grid is 362 MWh per year.
