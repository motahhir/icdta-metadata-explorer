The partial shading affects and reduces the output power produced by the system of solar P_V Array, and the V_I and P_I characteristics present more than one point of maximum power. To prevent the formation of hot spots in the panels and avoid the total loss of energy, bypass diodes are installed by manufacturers to prevent the formation of hot spots in the panels and avoid the total loss of energy. However, this solution is not effective and requires more investigation. As a result of the conduction of the diodes, several maximum points could appear. It then results in a complex algorithm of tracking the point of maximum power. In this paper, the effect of partial shading on the production of solar P_V array is studied and simulation results using MATLAB/SIMULINK software are presented. To minimize the effect of partial shading, some module configuration is proposed and their performances are evaluated. Finally, we propose an algorithm to detect a mismatch defect and correct it automatically to mitigate mismatch losses.
