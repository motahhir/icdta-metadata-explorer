Keywords: Advanced Driver Assistance Systems (ADAS), Autonomous vehicles, System verification, Test scenario, Automatic testing

The aim of this paper is to offer a novel way of creating a collection of automated scenario-based tests to evaluate the safety of autonomous cars, especially those that mix simulation and real-world testing. Our methodology is built on formal methods, which combine a mathematical model to describe a scenario, define a relevant metric, and generate a set of test scenarios for characterization of test scenarios with a formal situation description. However, any advanced driving system (ADS) may encounter an infinite number of situations and scenarios that influence its interaction. As a result, such scenarios can be characterized as an extensive collection of characteristics and parameters that define all potential scenarios and are investigated using a scenario-based method.
