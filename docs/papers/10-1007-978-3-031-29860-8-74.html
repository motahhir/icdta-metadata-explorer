Deadbeat Predictive Control Strategy of a Wind System Based on the DFIG Generator

Keywords: DFIG, Predictive Control, Deadbeat

This paper proposes a deadbeat predictive control scheme for a dual-fed asynchronous generator for variable speed wind power generation. The control proposed in this work aims at computing the voltage vector applied to the back-to-back converter to achieve the desired reference. A presentation of the system is first given, followed by a description of deadbeat control to apply it to both the rotor and the grid. Finally, a variable wind profile was applied to the system to verify the performance of the proposed control. The simulation results are presented in the MATLAB/Simulink framework and approved by the excellent trajectory tracking.
