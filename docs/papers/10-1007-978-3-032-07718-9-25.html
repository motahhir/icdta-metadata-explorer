Keywords: Information communication technology (ICT), E-management, Bibliometric review

The development of Information and Communication Technologies has influenced the course of many sectors, including education and management. E-management, intended as the set of practices that use ICT to optimize management processes, cannot be effectively adopted without studying individual perceptions and attitudes toward these technologies. This paper discusses a bibliometric analysis of publications published from 2019 to 2023, focusing on the topics of E-management, ICT, and perceptions or attitudes. It consists of journal articles, conference papers, and book chapters. It indicates an increasing interest in the convergence of ICT and management, the leading countries being Spain, India, and China, based on publication output. The current research points out the emergent trends, the main contributing institutions, and the importance of interdisciplinary research for a comprehensive interpretation of the influence of ICT on management practices and society.
