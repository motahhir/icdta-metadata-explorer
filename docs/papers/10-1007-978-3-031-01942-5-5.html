Keywords: Automatic speech recognition, Amazigh language, Artificial intelligence, Corpus, Formants frequency, Phoneme duration

Several studies in the development of Automatic Speech Recognition (ASR) systems have been yet presented and well studied for many languages. However, the particularities of the Amazigh Language due to its specific acoustic features such as the emphasis on Amazigh consonants pose many problems for developing its automatic speech recognition system. In this paper, we deal with the spoken recognition problem in Amazigh Language. We consider here a particular case of the problem, focusing on the phonemes formants frequencies which are a principal variable in specifying the phonetic substance of discourse sounds. We propose an approach allowing the vocal parameters analysis of phonetic units, as well as a recognition algorithm by analyzing the formants.
