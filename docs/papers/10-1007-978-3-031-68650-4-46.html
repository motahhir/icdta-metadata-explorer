The widespread adoption of Vehicle-to-Everything (V2X) communication systems has significantly transformed the electric vehicle (EV) charging infrastructure landscape. As V2X Chargers are essential for facilitating bidirectional power flow amid electric vehicles and the grid, efficiency and reliability of the power conversion process become paramount. This mini-review provides a comprehensive overview of recent advancements in DC-DC converters utilized in V2X chargers, focusing on the existing topologies and their features.
