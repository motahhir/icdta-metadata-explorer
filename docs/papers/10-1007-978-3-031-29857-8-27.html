Keywords: Internet of Things (IoT), Security, Trust Management (TM), Trust, Blockchain

Over the past few years, Internet of Things (IoT) has become one of the most influential innovative technologies in the world. A huge number of heterogeneous and dynamic devices work together to provide collaborative services and applications across various industries. Nevertheless, Trust Management systems remain an important task to be investigated since it leads to several challenges and issues. In this paper, Blockchain is presented as an emerging decentralized and distributed technology, with its key features make it the most suitable candidate to solve Trust Management issues in IoT. Furthermore, various research works related to Blockchain-based IoT Trust Management were discussed along with some open challenges and future directions.
