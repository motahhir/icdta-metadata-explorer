Keywords: Data Science, Artificial Intelligence, Quality Assurance, Quality practices, Learning Outcomes, Higher Education

The development of data science and artificial intelligence (DS/AI) will change the skills expected by society and the form of teaching (DS/AI) courses in higher education. This article proposes a Quality Assurance (QA) system adapted to teaching and learning DS/AI by integrating a framework of quality for learning and teaching DS/AI based on standards and references to measure learning outcomes. This system is designed to assure continuous improvement in teaching and learning DS/AI.
