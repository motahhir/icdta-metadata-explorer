Keywords: DFIM, DTC, EV, FOC, Switching table

Vehicles with thermal combustion engines that consume fossil fuels are very polluting because of their emissions of gases that harm the environment. Therefore, great importance is given to environment-friendly electric vehicles (EV). Currently, the control of the engine of EVs has a great importance in the EV industry, the DTC control is an attractive solution because of its many advantages, for this reason, this paper proposes a study of the DFIM controlled by the DTC strategy, in the case where it is used in the traction chain of electric vehicles. The modeling of the whole system is done in the Matlab/Simulink logiciel. This study shows that the DTC control strategy has advantages. In fact, it is simple to implement, and the speed of VE perfectly follows the speed set by the driver despite the non-linear variation of the load torque and despite the variation of the setpoint speed. However, the electromagnetic torque shows some ripples due to the hysteresis comparators used and the switching frequency.
