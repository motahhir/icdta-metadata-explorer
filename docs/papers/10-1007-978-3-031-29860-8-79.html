Keywords: Dynamic Wireless Power Transfer (DWPT), Electric Vehicle, Coil’s topologies, Misalignment, Electromagnetic coupling, Segmentation, Control

The global warming caused by the use of petroleum sources and the increase in its cost is major constraints in the field of transportation. To eliminate those problems we switched to electric vehicles (EV) but we have been faced with a lack of competitiveness due to energy storage. The dynamic Wireless Power Transfer solution will allow users and transport professionals to no longer worry about the autonomy of their vehicles and the power to be equipped with smaller, less expensive batteries. This paper presents the state of art of dynamic Wireless Power Transfer (DWPT) and proposes to find the optimal topology in the case of misalignment, the type of segmentation of the transmitter, and the type of control of this charging system.
