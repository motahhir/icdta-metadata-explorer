Keywords: Band stop filter, Metamaterial, Microstrip, Asymmetric split ring resonator (ASRR)

This work presents a design of a controllable bandstop microstrip filter based on an asymmetric split ring resonator (ASRR) . the proposed design is realized by a microstrip line loaded by ASRR. The structure of band-stop microstrip filters with ASRR is characterized by small size and simple structure. The ASRR offers the possibility of controlling the center frequency of the filter. These ASRR-based structures are thought to be of relevance for the creation of planar filters with possible use in future communication transceivers. The numerical and experimental results are in good agreement demonstrating the effectiveness of the proposed design.
