Over the last years, existing technologies have been applied to agricultural environments, resulting in new precision farming systems. Some of the multiple benefits of developing new agricultural technologies and applications include reduced costs associated with their construction and deployment, as well as more energy-efficient consumption. Therefore, agricultural precision systems focus on developing better, easier, cheaper, and overall more efficient ways to handle agricultural monitoring and actuation. In this project, all electronic components are powered by clean energy using photovoltaic panels and mini wind turbines. The Arduino card is chosen to design our measurement strategy of daily variables needed: humidity, temperature and pH ensured using a pH meter and DHT11. The application of this project in the field of agricultural monitoring.
