Keywords: Green hydrogen, Potential of hydrogen production, Hydrogen storage hybrid renewable energy system

Green energy is receiving increasing attention today, and researchers are examining different ways to develop it. Of these various forms, hydrogen is attractive as a central pillar of the energy transformation needed to limit global warming to two degrees Celsius, creating significant benefits for the environment, the energy system, and the global economy. Because hydrogen is a very light gas, its storage is a real challenge. Several methods of storing hydrogen as a gas at high pressure are possible. However, this requires technical means to stock it at low temperature or high pressure. The objective of this paper is to present the current situation of hydrogen production in the world, especially in Morocco, and to propose a solution to manage the energy flow in a hybrid renewable energy system using green hydrogen. This gas is an opportunity for a strong integration of renewable energy in the energy mix.
