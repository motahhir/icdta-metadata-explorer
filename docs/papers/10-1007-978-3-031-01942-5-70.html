Keywords: Wind turbine control, T-S fuzzy modelling, Continuous-time model, Predictive controller (CMPC)

The purpose of this paper is to look into the design and implementation of a wind turbine system. Because the wind turbine system's linearized model is required for control synthesis, the Takagi-Suengo fuzzy technique is used to generate a robust linear model, which is then used to design the continuous-time model predictive controller (CMPC). The simulation results demonstrate the effectiveness of the suggested approach for variable speed wind turbine control.
