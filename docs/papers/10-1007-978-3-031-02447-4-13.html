Keywords: Chatbot, Data quality, Deep learning, Education, Human-machine Interaction

In recent years, the size of NLP models, the number of parameters they use, and their performances have gotten all the attention. The race for gigantism increases the score of the models at the expense of the environment and high energy consumption. However, the quality of the data with which they interact influences the reliability of these models. To our knowledge, this is the first paper to investigate the influence of misspelling on question-answering agent responses. It is an important aspect of chatbot seen as a whole and complex system with its dynamic relations and interactions. According to the results, the R-NET model performs well against misspelling.
