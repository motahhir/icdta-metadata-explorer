Intelligent IoT Platform for Precocious Detection of Late Blight and TYLCV Tomato Disease in Morocco

Keywords: Tomato diseases, Convolutional neural networks, IoT

The agricultural sector is a key backbone of Morocco’s economy. Agriculture has played a major role in the economy through agricultural exports and job creation, thus improving and maintaining good farming practices is critical in ensuring great agricultural yields. Tomatoes are a major product in Moroccan agricultural exports (51%). However, tomato plants are prone to diseases that can significantly reduce production volumes. The two main diseases to which tomato plants in Morocco are exposed are late blight and TYLCV (Tomato yellow leaf curl virus). The objective of this paper is to present an intelligent IoT platform that uses convolutional neural networks to ensure precocious detection of these two diseases, and thus limit the damage they could cause. The proposed convolutional neural networks model allows a 98% accuracy.
