Keywords: UAV, Drone, Early fire detection, Wildfire monitoring, Camera, Image analysis

Due to the rising occurrence and strength of wildfires, especially in wooded regions, it is crucial to have quick and effective fire monitoring systems. The objective of this paper is to create and execute a drone tailored for prompt fire detection in Morocco. The suggested plan includes using a drone with a high-quality camera, gas detector, and advanced image analysis algorithms. This drone is created to improve authorities’ response to wildfire incidents by offering real-time surveillance and fire detection capabilities. The approach involves thorough analysis of the system, adherence to engineering standards, and extensive field testing to assess the drone's performance in real-life situations.
