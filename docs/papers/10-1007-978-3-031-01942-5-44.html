Keywords: Firefly algorithm, Meixner polynomial, Meixner moments, 2D images reconstruction

Discrete orthogonal polynomials are widely used for image processing, these polynomials are characterized by parameters. However, it was very important to optimize these parameters to obtain a good result, in this context; this study suggests a new approach to optimize Meixner polynomial (MPs) parameters (β, u) based on the Firefly Algorithm (FA), and we use them to reconstruct 2D images. Using the FA algorithm determines the optimal parameters of the MPs, which improves the quality of the reconstruction. The simulation results, on the one hand, suggest that the proposed method based on the FA algorithm offers a high image reconstruction quality. On other hand, the comparison carried out with other algorithms indicates the advantage of the proposed method.
