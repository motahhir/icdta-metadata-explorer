Keywords: Digital Transformation, Information Systems, Performance

A strategic necessity in today's academic setting is the broad acceptance of digital transformation. It has a profound impact on economies and society while giving businesses the opportunity to increase productivity, boost competitiveness, and streamline operations. Additionally, it increases investment and enhances the standard of living for citizens by promoting connectivity and digital inclusion. Moroccan universities face a formidable struggle in this dynamic environment. Digitalization emerges as a critical means of improving their overall performance. It also paves the way for the creation of Universities 4.0, which are inclusive, high-achieving, networked, and intelligent universities. But before such institutions can be founded, the dematerialization process and the digital and numerical infrastructure need to be mastered. The study looks at the actual digital transformation that Moroccan public institutions are going through and wonders if using these strategies could make them more successful.
