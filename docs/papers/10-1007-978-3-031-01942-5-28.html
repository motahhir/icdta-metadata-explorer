Keywords: Internet of Things, IoT, Models, Protocols, Z-Wave, Wifi, Bluetooth, UML

The Internet of Things (IoT) is a hot topic. It considers a system of billions of physical objects, industrial machines, and billions of humans where these objects connected to the Internet. The Internet of Things based on existing communication techniques and protocols. The diversity of protocols responds to the multiplicity of billions of connected objects. There are different technologies, each of which has its own particularities in terms of a set of criteria. However, the absence of connectivity protocol results in a lack of interoperability between IoT objects and applications. In this article we answer the question of protocol operation, we will present the operating models of medium-range network protocols. These models ensure a deep and flexible conception of how network protocols work using UML modeling. We used the activity diagram.it permit to graphically represent the behavior of these protocols. In this paper, we focus on medium-range are Z-wave, Wi-Fi, and Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE).
