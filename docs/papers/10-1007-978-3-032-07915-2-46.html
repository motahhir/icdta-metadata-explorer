Tourism sectors under recovery from the COVID-19 pandemic learn more about AI to improve operational efficiency and customer experience. It, however, has employment, data security, and ethical challenges. This paper intends to analyze the application of AI in tourism, synthesizing recent studies from 2011 to 2024 for trend identification and strengths and weaknesses analysis. It highlights the applications of AI, the challenges encountered, and the opportunities for integration, thereby galvanizing future research toward the effective, ethical, and sustainable use of AI in the field.
