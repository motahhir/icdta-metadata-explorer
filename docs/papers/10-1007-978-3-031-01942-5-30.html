Keywords: Design science research, IoT platform, Architecture evaluation, Quality factors, Smart healthcare

The Internet of Things (IoT) has become a major technological revolution. Evaluating any IoT advancements comprehensively is critical to understand the conditions under which they can be more useful, as well as to assess the robustness and efficiency of IoT systems to validate them before their deployment in real life. The compliance of IoT architecture to quality is paramount to achieving users’ satisfaction. Currently, we do not have a comprehensive set of guidelines to appraise and select the most suitable IoT platform architectures that meet relevant criteria. Considering the challenges associated with developing IoT healthcare applications. This paper proposes a reference platform as a quality guideline to help both researchers and practitioners who are developing and maintaining IoT platforms. We focus on the suggestion of quality factors to evaluate IoT-Based Healthcare Architecture. We adopted the design science research approach to develop a novel evaluation.
