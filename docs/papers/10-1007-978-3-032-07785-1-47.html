Keywords: Endogenous growth, Institutional approach, Linear regression

The main objective of this article is to determine the governance indica-tors that can boost economic growth in Morocco. To do this, we have tried to integrate an institutional indicator, such as the digital governance variable, and other economic indicators, such as gross domestic product and investment, into a growth model by estimating them using the linear regression method based on World Bank data for the period two thousand and twenty twenty-one (2000–2021). This allowed us to note that certain governance variables such as Internet access have a positive impact on economic growth in Morocco. As a result, we have confirmed the hypothesis of the endogenous growth model, according to which technical progress can promote growth, and that of Mauro's model (1995), which claims that governance has a positive influence on economic growth and long-term investment. This analysis could positively influence the decision-making of stakeholders.
