Keywords: Artificial intelligence, educational innovation, personalized learning, learning analytics

Artificial intelligence (AI) has become an integral part of human daily lives, leaving no area of life without affecting it in one way or another. Whether it is sport, health, renewable energies, business, transport, and the field of education has not been spared by this revolution. AI can revolutionize the education system's way of working and learning, opening new possibilities for learners and simplifying various educational tasks, ultimately making the learning process simple and hassle-free. Today's artificial intelligence enhances the ability to advance education by providing personalized learning experiences for each student based on their unique learning styles, interests, and skills. This paper discusses specifically the most prominent emerging applications in the field of education in the era of artificial intelligence, such as predicting academic guidance, detecting learning styles, anti-cheating in exams, predicting students’ performance, intelligent tutoring systems, etc. This research aims to present the education system's current state under AI and shed light on future applications and challenges from both a theoretical and a practical perspective.
