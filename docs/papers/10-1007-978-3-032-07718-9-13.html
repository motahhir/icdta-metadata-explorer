Keywords: Three phase wireless power transfer, Battery electric vehicle, CC charging, Compensation network

This paper presents a comprehensive study on the design of precise power flow regulation technique for three-phase wireless power transfer (WPT) systems applied to electric vehicle (EV) battery charging. The background to this paper is the emergence and development of charging stations for electric vehicles (EVs), and wireless charging solutions in particular. In this paper, the control of the wireless charger is analyzed, in order to evaluate its performance when testing different charging methods using the three-phase WPT. Simulation scenarios are carried out to confirm the robustness and adaptability of the control strategies. This kind of study helps to advance the WPT, especially the three-phase for high operating powers.
