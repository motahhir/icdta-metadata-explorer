Keywords: Industry 4.0, Moroccan companies, IoT, challenges for industry 4.0

Industry 4.0 (I4.0) is revolutionizing the world, especially the manufacturing companies which face challenges to integrate new technologies in technical and non-technical services. The I4.0 is driven by the digitization, rise of data and connectivity, analytics, and human-machine interaction, therefore its implementation is a complex process. Morocco and many countries are currently implementing policies to facilitate companies’ transformation within the I4.0. The primary objective of this pilot study is to provide a first insight about the implementation of I4.0 in Moroccan companies. This paper presents a case study of a company that have implemented the I4.0 technologies within 15 Moroccan companies. The collected data is analysed to provide insight into the status of implementation of the I4.0 technologies, with a focus on identifying barriers.
