Mutual Coupling Reduction in Array Antenna Using a New EBG Structure

Keywords: Antenna array, Mutual coupling, EBG structure

The goal of this study is to use Electromagnetic Band Gap (EBG) structures to reduce reciprocal coupling between the radiating elements of an antenna array. Initially, the Mushroom-like EBG structure was used as a reference structure in this field. Second, in comparison to the Mushroom-like EBG structure, a new beneficial EBG structure was used. The proposed New EBG (NEBG) structure’s interest is demonstrated by a comparison of the outcomes obtained in each situation. Two separate simulators, HFSS and CST MWS were used to simulate all of the structures. The findings we achieved by the two methods are nearly identical for each structure.
