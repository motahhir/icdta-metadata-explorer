Parametric Identification by Improved Levenberg-Marquardt Method of Solar Cell’s Double Diode Model

Keywords: Parametric identification, Single diode model, Double diode model, Improved Levenberg-Marquardt method

The study of a photovoltaic module response is ensured by analyzing the behavior of its solar cells. To do this, the identification of the structural parameters of the solar cells is primordial. In literature, we distinguish several electrical models of solar cells which we consider the single and the double diode models. In this paper, we use an improved method “Levenberg-Marquardt combined with simulated annealing” to determine the five and the seven electrical parameters of the considered models. The performance of the proposed method is evaluated by comparing its identification accuracy with other methods discussed in the literature. A comparative study shows the better accuracy provided by the double diode model compared to the single diode model.
