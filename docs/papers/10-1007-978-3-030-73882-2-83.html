In this paper, we highlight the technology of the fifth generation of mobile communications (5G), its impact on human health and its association with the emergence of the Corona virus in China. We have found through this study, that this technology uses frequencies below 6 GHz used also in other previous generations of mobile networks and they do not lead to the emergence of dangerous diseases like cancer or the corona virus, like what some people claim, and it turned out that this virus comes from the pangolin, which transmitted the infection to humans in Wuhan, China, in late 2019.
