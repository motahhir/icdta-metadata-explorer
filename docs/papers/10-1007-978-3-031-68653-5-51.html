Keywords: Digital transformation, health sector, telemedicine, Fez-Meknes region, Morocco

As digital transformation has grown significantly globally, all sectors are affected. The health sector is also concerned by the use of several forms of digitalization. Among the forms of digitalization in the health sector, we find telemedicine procedures. Our article seeks to study the situation of telemedicine as a form of digital transformation in a specific region, that of Fez-Meknes in Morocco. This issue has not been previously addressed in the region, knowing that it is of great magnitude. The results show that telemedicine is increasingly being used and could help patients access care more easily.
