Keywords: Refugee crisis, Inventory management, Refugee supply chain management, Humanitarian logistics, Disaster management

This paper aims to prepare a set of the most important assumptions to be considered when modeling the inventory management of a large humanitarian refugee organization. For this purpose, our paper attempts to link the results of conceptual works regarding refugee crisis management to the principles of inventory management in the context of the humanitarian supply chain. As a result, the paper paves the way for researchers to develop an efficient mathematical model to take under consideration the various parameters, such as the big demand fluctuations, the complexity of the situation of refugees, the choices of shipments, the budget, the optimal partitioning, and finally to the imperatives concerning the subsequent solution approaches.
