Control of a Bi-axial Solar Tracker by Observing the Power Supplied by the MPPT

Keywords: Photovoltaic panel, Solar tracker, Solar radiation, Mppt

The solar tracker is a mechanism that helps the photovoltaic panel maximize its efficiency during the day while keeping it oriented towards direct solar radiation. To manipulate the movement of the solar tracker, most use a solar collector that specifies the position of the sun before operating the motors. The idea of this paper is to replace the solar collector with an observation system of the PV output power (between the PV and the MPPT). The follower control card observes the variation of this power as long as it is increased. The system continues the correction of the tracking and it stops only if it detects a decrease in this power. In this way, we will have a perfect follow-up that looks for the peak of the power provided by the photovoltaic panel.
