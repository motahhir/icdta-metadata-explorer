MPPT Algorithm of Piezoelectric Power Generation System Based on Nonlinear Extrapolation

Keywords: Vibrational energy recovery, Bond graph methodology, Suspension model, Piezoelectric harvester, Energy harvesting

This paper deals with the electricity generation from the biodynamics piezoelectric harvesting system using the local stepwise optimization algorithm. However, the sensitivity error of the maximum power point tracking (MPPT) algorithm based on nonlinear extrapolation has been established in this work to adjust the circuit impedance in real-time and to achieve the impedance target load matching. The aforementioned approach provided the MPPT topology based on nonlinear extrapolation for the harvester system. Moreover, the driving stability through robust active front steering and active differential control is enhanced. Simulations models of electric power generation for piezoelectric vibration energy harvesting are proposed. The proposed algorithm’s feasibility surpassed the classical observation method in different disturbance and working conditions.
