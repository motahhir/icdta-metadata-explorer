The Effect of FDI and Technological Innovation on Morocco: Modeling and Prediction with Artificial Intelligence

Keywords: Foreign direct investment, Time series, Artificial intelligence, Economic growth, Technological innovation

This study explores the role of Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) and technological innovation in the Moroccan economy, with a focus on artificial intelligence (AI) and time-series forecasting. In a context of rapid globalization, the attractiveness of FDI is essential for Morocco, which invests in advanced technologies to strengthen its attractiveness. The methodology is based on a multiple regression analyzing the links between innovation, economic forecasting, and FDI, based on a questionnaire sent to various Moroccan economic actors. The results show that AI and reliable forecasting increase economic stability and boost investor confidence. However, there are limitations, such as the small sample size and the lack of international comparison. To maximize FDI, it is recommended to strengthen digital infrastructure and local technological skills, while encouraging public–private partnerships. These perspectives provide a solid basis for future research oriented towards Morocco's sustainable economic competitiveness.
