Keywords: V2X charger, Bidirectional dc-dc power converter, Nonlinear modeling, Backstepping-based controller

This article treats the issue of controlling a dc-dc V2X (vehicle-to-everything) charger for electric vehicles. Two operating modes are distinguished for the dc-dc charger: charging mode and discharging mode. This work aims to establish a control law that will guarantee the safe charging and discharging of the battery electric vehicle (BEV) during G2V (grid-to-vehicle) mode and V2G (vehicle-to-grid) mode, respectively. To achieve this goal, the overall investingated system includes a bidirectional dc-dc power converter connected to a battery, is modeled, and a backstepping-based controller is designed. Numerical simulations are provided to demonstrate the suggested control strategy's efficiency.
