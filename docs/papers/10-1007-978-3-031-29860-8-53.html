Keywords: Islamic art, geometric art, star, rosette, tiling, polygon, symmetry group

The construction of the tiling is a crucial step for the construction of Islamic geometric patterns by the polygonal method. However, the majority of the works based on the polygonal method ignore this step. Hence, the researchers try to extract tessellations from existing geometric patterns. Therefore, it would be useful to develop a method to build a wide variety of tilings. This broadens the spectrum of constructed geometric patterns based on the Polygons In Contact (PIC) method. The goal of this paper is to provide guidelines for generating periodic tilings of the symmetry groups pmm and cmm. The proposed method is based on the symmetry group theory.
