Keywords: Blockchain, Finance, Fintech

This article delves into the revolutionary impact of blockchain on the financial sector, tracing its evolution from conceptual origins in the 1980s to practical implementation with Bitcoin in the 2000s. The literature review meticulously analyzes the advantages of blockchain, specifically focusing on its implications within the Moroccan financial landscape. Employing a systematic review methodology, the study deeply explores 21 relevant articles. The ensuing discussion revolves around four key pillars of blockchain: the reimagining of financial trust, a cost revolution in finance, the disruption of Fintech, and the redefinition of intermediary roles. Throughout, blockchain emerges as a transformative force, offering innovative solutions to the challenges faced by the financial sector, encompassing decentralization, enhanced transparency, and the automation of processes. This comprehensive examination positions blockchain as a dynamic catalyst for positive change in the financial industry.
