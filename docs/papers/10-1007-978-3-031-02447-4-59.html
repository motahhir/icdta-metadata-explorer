The State-of-the-Art of Safe Fly Zone for a Power Transmission Lines Inspection UAV

Keywords: Unmanned Aerial Vehicle, Electromagnetic interferences, Safe fly zone

In this paper, we present a state of the art of Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV) safe distance calculation methods. After the presentation of the advantages of using UAVs in the inspection of high voltage power lines, we present the effects of electromagnetic interference generated by these lines on the good functioning of UAVs and the proper conduct of these operations. Considering the problems caused by electromagnetic interference on the UAV's electronic circuits and communication, it is necessary to define a safe distance that determines the UAV no-fly zone. Several calculation methods exist to determine the value of this distance, which depends on different parameters, to integrate them into planning algorithms of power line inspection UAVs to ensure equipment safety and operation success.
