Keywords: Antenna, 5G, Filtenna, n258 band

A 5G Filtenna (filtering-Antenna) was described in this paper, it can operate at the n258 band (24.25–27.5 GHz). The structure was designed on an FR4 Epoxy substrate which has an electrical size of 0.52λ0 × 0.52λ0 × 0.087λ0, noting that, λ0 is the free-space wavelength at the resonant frequency 26.17 GHz. The software used for the simulation is ANSYS HFSS, based on the finite element approach. The simulation results show an omnidirectional radiation pattern, the reflection coefficient is less than 10 dB |S11| < 10 dB, which means that our system captures at least 90% of the input power and just 10% of the power is lost due to reflection. The achieved selectivity reinforces the performance of our Filtenna as well as the (Voltage Standing Wave Ratio) VSWR result which is less than 2.
