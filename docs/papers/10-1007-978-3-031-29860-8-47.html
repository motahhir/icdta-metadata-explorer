Keywords: face recognition, face identification, face verification, biometric

Biometrics involves face recognition for recognizing a human’s identity. It is exceedingly used in many fields, for example, computer vision, image analysis, and security applications. This paper contains five sections. The first section presents an overview of face recognition technology, and why this biometric modality is important. The second section describes the main stages of face recognition. The third section highlights the different methods used for face recognition including holistic, local, and hybrid. The next section summarizes some popular datasets available for face recognition. At the end, the paper concludes with a note on future research.
