Generation and Analysis of Residential Microgrid Database

Keywords: Microgrid, Database, Data pre-processing

Nowadays, the high level of solar PV rooftop penetration leads to the increase of residential microgrids and brings new challenges mainly to the Energy Management Systems (EMS). To investigate such issues, a Microgrid Platform (MGP) has been developed in the National Engineers School of Tunis (ENIT). As all residential microgrids, MGP provides a large amount of data from various types of equipment and devices. Each of these data has its format and sampling period and is accessible via its dedicated portal requiring authentication. Hence, the analysis and exploitation of such microgrid data are tedious. In this paper, we propose the design of a centralized database that can be used for Microgrid analysis and EMS development. The data formats are standardized according to the chosen database management system (DBMS), namely MySQL. Depending on the user’s needs, unified sampling periods for all the microgrid data are implemented. In addition, access to the proposed database is designed to be possible from a unique portal.
