Keywords: Wind energy, Induction motor, PMSG, Input output linearization, Centrifugal hydraulic pump, Water pumping system

To solve the problem of water for irrigation in isolated regions and rural remote areas, where the expansion of the power network line is complex and she is more costly. In this paper a control of the wind pumping systems is presented. Classical control strategy of induction motors (IMs) is very sensitive to the variation of rotor resistance, to leads a strong coupling between the rotor flux and the electromagnetic torque. To resolve this difficulty, we propose in this paper, a nonlinear control of high performance applied of induction motor, to improve the tracking control, for guarantee stability, robustness to parameter variations, perfect decoupling between the flux and torque. The robustness of the proposed study is confirmed through software simulation with parameter variations.
