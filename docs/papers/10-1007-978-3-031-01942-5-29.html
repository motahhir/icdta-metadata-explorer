For a standard software-defined Network (SDN), the controller class the flux gotten for the switch to using it in the network virtualization platform (NFV). we wanted to create a new IoT architecture forced with SDN while adding NFV to the equation. And then we see that the IoT is taking the development between the internet and the new connected devices in our everyday life to another level. Therefore, to be more effective and secure, it needs new approaches and new architecture designs to keep a good level of performance and security. Our paper presents a developed IoT architecture design with the use of SDN technology and also possibilities to use NFV and how it’s going to all work!
