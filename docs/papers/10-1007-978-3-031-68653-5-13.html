Keywords: Image encryption, Security, Image decryption, XOR operator, RSA Algorithm

A popular solution is cryptography, which is a reliable and secure data protection technique due to the rise in information access terminals and telecommunications. XOR and RSA cryptosystems are used in this study to present a data protection tool that is popular. In this method, an XOR function is executed between a randomly generated mask and the four channels of an RGB image (alpha, red, green, and blue), then RSA encryption is utilized. The results prove that the method is effective.
