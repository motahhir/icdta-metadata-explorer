Due to the high cost of fossil fuels and environmental concerns, the number of renewable energy sources is growing rapidly. Supported by decentralized power generation, renewable energy is growing rapidly, mitigating the challenges of global climate change and reducing reliance on fossil fuels. Otherwise, the combination of renewable energy sources in the grid will have a significant impact on the performance, reliability, and stability of the grid. For this reason, in particular, to overcome voltage errors, improve frequency response, improve network installation and solve many technical problems associated with the integration of these types of production systems into distribution networks. Advanced control technology. You need to do this. The focus of this article is to explore the different approaches to managing renewable energy in microgrids. Below is a brief description of a comparative analysis of these control methods.
