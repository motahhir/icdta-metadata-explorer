Keywords: digital resource, learning styles, flipped classroom, learning technique

The use of information and communication technologies (ICT) in the flipped classroom is covered in the article, with an emphasis on how it improves student performance and engagement. More flexibility is provided by encouraging students to participate actively in distance learning activities. According to the findings, the flipped classroom approach outperforms conventional techniques in terms of performance, primarily because it makes use of customized instructional videos. The essay highlights the requirement of tailoring instruction to each student's needs and calls for more study in the area of ICT and education to enhance teaching methods and student outcomes.
