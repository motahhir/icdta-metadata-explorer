Keywords: Smart tutoring system, Collaborative metacognitive, Learning, Learning process

The smart tutoring system is a system that helps learners to solve their exercises. To improve this system, an integration of collaborative technique is required. This tool, aid learners to understand very well their courses and resolve the difficulties of case studies that are based on team work. The aim of this paper is to fix the problem of the implicit tutor in the smart tutoring system and propose the integration of collaborative metacognitive technique as a solution. The process starts with a collaborative design phase and finished with the implementation phase using activate storyline 360 environment.
