Improved Performance of Wind Turbines Based on Doubly Fed Generator Using the IFOC-SVM Control

Keywords: DFIG, IFOC, SVM

The vectorial command abridged, with its two forms, direct DFOC, and indirect IFOC is one of the valuable controls for controlling the asynchronous doubly fed generator. However, the disadvantage of this control lies in its low robustness against parametric variations, which results in a degradation of the control performance. To remedy these problems, a development of the indirect vector control of IFOC powers by applying the different voltage vectors of the SVM inverter is presented. This improvement helps to solve the problem of parameter sensitivity, minimizes the harmonics of the voltages applied to the machine, and reduces the oscillations of the output values. The efficiency of this control is demonstrated by simulation results on the Matlab/Simulink environment.
