Keywords: Photovoltaic system, Boost power converter, Incremental conductance algorithm, Irradiance and temperature real measurements

In this paper, we propose a study of a boost converter in photovoltaic system integrated into a smart microgrid. The research involves non-linear output characteristics of a panel PV using the real measurements radiation and temperature levels in Marrakech on December 1st, 2024. The incremental conductance MPPT control algorithm is tested and assessed under changing operating points, with specific attention to the effect of a restore inductance filter. Numerical simulations analyze the proposed model focusing on its speed, accuracy, and stability.
