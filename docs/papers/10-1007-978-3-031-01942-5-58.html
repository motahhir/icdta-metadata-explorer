Keywords: Energy harvester, Photovoltaic, Solar cell, Indoor energy, Neural Network

An energy harvester is the best way to avoid having to use primary and secondary batteries. Thermal, vibration, solar, and wind energy are among the energy collection methods being investigated by researchers. Indoor light, which is a mix of artificial light and scattered sunlight, can be collected using solar cells. Despite the minimal power (in microwatts) generated, the energy can be used to power wireless sensor nodes. This research looks at a number of energy harvesting approaches and explores the need for indoor energy harvesting as well as the challenges that it presents..
