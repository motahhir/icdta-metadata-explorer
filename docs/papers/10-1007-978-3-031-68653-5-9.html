Data collection is a crucial step in various fields, which helps to obtain valuable insights and make better decisions. However, finding an open dataset that matches a user’s needs is one of the biggest challenges, particularly with advances in artificial intelligence that depend on data for algorithms. In the educational field, assignments are essential in virtual and traditional environments. In this paper, we aim to address the lack of educational datasets, particularly for assignments, which on the one hand help teachers evaluate learners’ progress. On the other hand, they help learners apply and practice their understanding as an intermediate between theoretical knowledge and practical application. Furthermore, the data search process applied was based on keywords as a search style to achieve this objective.
