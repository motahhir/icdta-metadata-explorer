Keywords: DC to DC converter, Buck-boost, Multiple input single output, Universal Input Module

The conversion of source power into useable power is required when using renewable energy straight from the point of generation. The DC-to-DC conversion for Smart House emphasises this work's unique scope. Because the voltage and current of renewable energy sources change very much, a DC-to-DC converter with a large input range is necessary. Physical isolation, a long lifespan, and exposure to the elements need safe and dependable end-product criteria. The need to reach a wide range of customers necessitates a product that does not require any fiddling to get up and to run but can be used for the great majority of energy sources right out of the box. A DC-DC converter called a Universal Input Module (UIM) serves as the initial step between the energy source and the useable power bus. A Multiple Input Single Output (MISO) system may be created by connecting UIMs in a parallel configuration. A four-switch buck-boost controller is the heart of the modification. All key components, surrounding components, and analogous simulation circuits are included in the work. The simulation results produced by LTSpice meet the requirements of the customer.
