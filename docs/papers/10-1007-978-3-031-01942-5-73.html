This paper proposes designing and realising a Fire Safety System for Controlling and Monitoring a Siren using a new Embedded Board to solve fire control and extinguishing at the Smart Farm level. The new embedded fire safety system is developed by designing and modelling an embedded board called F3SF (Fire Safety System Smart Farm) based on the Atmega328p Microcontroller. It ensures the monitoring of the Smart Farm in the event of a fire by using specific sensors that detect the fire linked to the proposed board, which automatically controls and monitors (automatic maintenance of the siren and the sprinklers) the siren and sprinklers used to extinguish the fire. The proposed Fire Safety System implemented in the new embedded board F3SF-0X using a voltage sensor, an H-bridge, an ATMega328p microcontroller, a Flame Sensor, an MQ-135 gas sensor, a Water sprinkler, and an electronic relay. The new system is simulated in the EasyEDA web software tool.
